About

Researcher in physical oceanography and regional coupled Earth-system modeling.

I work on high-resolution atmosphere-ocean coupled models, hybrid atmosphere-ocean systems, and applications that connect ocean dynamics, local meteorology, air quality, climate, and marine biogeochemistry. My current work focuses on the Northeast U.S. Continental Shelf and the physical and biogeochemical impacts of offshore wind energy.

Research Activities

  • Impacts of Offshore Wind Energy on the Northeast U.S. Continental Shelf (2025-): developing a coupled atmosphere-ocean-biogeochemistry model based on WRF, ROMS, and Darwin to investigate offshore wind farm impacts on ocean currents, local mixing, and marine biogeochemistry.
  • Agricultural Fallowing, Dust Emissions, and Air-Quality Hazards (2024-): using WRF-Chem and high-resolution land-use data to investigate how agricultural fallowing and drought-driven land-surface changes influence dust emissions, visibility degradation, and air-quality hazards in California's Central Valley.
  • Agricultural Irrigation Impacts on Local Meteorology and Air Quality (2022-): developed a WRF-irrigation model for California's Central Valley that serves as the next-generation baseline forecasting model in the California Air Resources Board air quality forecasting system.
  • Hybrid Atmosphere-Ocean Coupled Modeling (2021-): developed a ROMS-based hybrid coupled model with a statistical atmospheric model to investigate multi-scale atmosphere-ocean interactions and biogeochemical feedbacks associated with ENSO in the tropical Pacific.
  • High-Resolution Atmosphere-Ocean Coupled Modeling (2018-2022): investigated eddy-mean flow interactions during Super Typhoon Muifa rapid intensification and hurricane-induced remote extreme precipitation with 1 km and 4 km WRF-ROMS coupled models.
  • Diurnal Effects in Marginal Seas (2016-2020): developed a three-dimensional ROMS regional circulation model to study diurnal surface-forcing impacts on seasonal temperature and salinity variability in the East China Sea.
